Day of Week

Saturday dominates at 14.5 mi avg. Weekends = 34% of career miles.

Time of Day

55% afternoon. 15% pre-dawn = race starts + early training blocks.

Seasonal Observations

Fall Peak

October is consistently the highest-volume month — race season in the desert. 4 of the top 10 months are October.

Summer Dip

June–July often drops, especially in non-mountain-race years. Arizona heat + travel to altitude races.

December Spike

Desert Solstice anchors December — 3 of the top 5 longest runs are in December.

Spring Ramp

March is the build month — 2024's 609-mile March was the single highest month ever recorded.
